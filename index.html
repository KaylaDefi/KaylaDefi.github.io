About Me

As a certified software developer, I love building websites, applications, and small projects that experiment with using code to animate. My expertise lies in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), though I am rapidly working to expand my knowledge of other languages. I am currently developing a cutting-edge dapp using Next.js, with integrations into the Solana blockchain. Dedicated to continuous learning, I strive to continuously grow my full-stack development capabilities.

I am passionate about blockchain technology and am fascinated by its potential to transform and disrupt, shifting paradigms and reshaping industries. My interests extend beyond just decentralized finance to explore other critical aspects of decentralization. I am actively delving into decentralized storage solutions that allow for more secure, distributed data management, as well as decentralized identity verification using zk-SNARKs (Zero-Knowledge Proofs) to ensure privacy and security without compromising personal information. These innovations, I believe, are key to enabling a more transparent, equitable, and autonomous future.

I am open to collaborations and embrace new opportunities that challenge me to grow further. I love hearing new ideas and discussing theory. Need help building a website or application? Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions or comments.
